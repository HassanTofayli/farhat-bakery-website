Product Details

Bread slicers save time, labor cost and expense. Our high quality gravity fed bread slicers are fast and efficient. The standard slicing thickness is 12mm or 14mm but we can customize it to our customers’ requirements, from 8mm to 30mm. Made with high quality stainless steel parts and imported cutting blades, our slicers are smooth, fast and efficient. They can also be fitted with an emergency stop switch, which notably improves safety during operation.
